Email Extractor (Sheets) Privacy Policy


Why the Add-on Needs Permission to Access your Google Account

The Email Extractor (Sheets) add-on requires access to certain services in your Google Account. Here it is explained why these permissions are needed and how the add-on manages your data.

Google Add-ons Permissions Explained

The add-on is available inside a Google Spreadsheet, and you manage all the settings from inside Google Sheets. It does not read or delete any spreadsheet in your Google Drive.

The add-on lets you select any Gmail label (folder) from the list of available labels and extracts all emails inside that label to your Google Sheets. It only reads the list of your Gmail labels and does not delete or modify any label.

The add-on needs access to your email to read the message body, the header and extract any emails that fit the condition selected by you (“All Emails”, By a specific label or the “Newest Emails”) found in the email to your Google Sheet. The add-on only reads your email and does not delete or send any email.

The add-on is available inside your Google Spreadsheet contained in a sidebar. You can manage all the rules and manually run the download process from the sidebar. The “Retrieve Emails from a specific Label” also uses custom HTML codes to provide a better user experience.

The add-on accesses your Google Drive to download attachments (if you choose the option) but does not delete any existing files in your Google Drive.

Email Extractor (Sheets) - Privacy Policy

- The add-on will not collect your information without your consent; it only collects the minimum amount of necessary information to operate the add-on.

- No humans will read any of your data, including email messages and attachments.

- The add-on will not store your data on non-Google servers. All your settings are stored on Google servers inside the property store of the Google project associated with the add-on.

- The add-on will not use any of your data for market research, advertising, serving personalized content, retargeting, or recommendations.

- The add-on will use access to read Gmail message bodies (including attachments), metadata, headers, and settings to extract email properties/fields selected by you. Your data will not be used for any other purpose.

- The add-on also will only use access to read and write into your Google Drive. The add-on does not delete any files from your Google Drive. Your Google Drive would only be accessed if you selected to download your attachments into Google Drive. Your data will not be used for any other purpose.

- There will not transfer or share your information with others unless necessary to provide and improve these features and comply with applicable law.
